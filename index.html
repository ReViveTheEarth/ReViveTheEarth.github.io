About ReVive

A circular platform with an earthy heartbeat.

ReVive is a recycling organization built to feel like the future while staying rooted in the planet beneath our feet. We map local materials, route them to ethical processors, and surface your impact in a single, living dashboard.

Instead of tossing “trash” into a black box, ReVive shows you exactly how your plastics, metals, and e-waste flow back into new products, keeping resources in motion and out of landfills.

  • Community drop-off hubs
  • Partner logistics network

How it works

A simple flow for complex waste streams.

We keep it intuitive on the surface and intelligent underneath. Every step is tracked, timestamped, and rolled into that live counter you see on the home screen.

01 — Scan & sort
Drop off at a ReVive hub

Bring plastics, metals, cardboard, e-waste and more. Our signage & smart scales guide you to place each item in the right stream for accurate weight tracking.

02 — Route & recover
We move materials intelligently

We batch materials and route them to vetted recyclers. Each shipment is logged so your contribution flows straight into the live ReVive impact ledger.

03 — Visualize & share
Watch the counter rise

Your kilograms update the global ReVive counter and your personal dashboard. Share your milestones and invite others to amplify the impact.
